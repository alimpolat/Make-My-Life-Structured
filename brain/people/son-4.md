4-year-old son. Very active toddler. Needs constant attention, play, routine, and security. Can't advocate for himself — his needs are non-negotiable.

Coach Elena's approach: Daily 15 minutes of undivided, phone-free floor time (his choice of activity). Bedtime routine is sacred: bath, story, cuddle, same sequence every night. Involve him in "helping" with cooking, cleaning, carrying things. Active play with him IS exercise (Marcus agrees).
